Brad Stemke

Front End Developer & Designer

Optimist . Adventurer . Collector

I like to code, and I like what coding can create. Working with people to help shape and bring their ideas to life on the web is something I really enjoy, and I do that with HTML, CSS/Sass, JS, PHP, and Liquid.

I maintain several collections at home, the main one being my collection of found audio, mostly in the form of discarded cassette tapes. I am building an online archive of my audio collection, which can be found here, Lonely With Noise.

When I am not at home, I am usually out thrifting or at an estate sale, having a burger, spending time with good friends, or driving somewhere new to explore. These days are documented in photos with my iPhone & Fuji Disposable cameras.

If you feel I may be able to help you with a project, you are probably right and you should say hello.
