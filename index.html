Hello, my name is Brad. I'd like to think of myself a few different ways, but mainly as an optimist, adventurer, and a collector.

When I am at home, I am filling my time by baking oatmeal chocolate chip cookies, working on an embroidery project, furthering my knowledge of web development, or working on my collection of found audio, mainly in the form of discarded cassettes. I am building an online archive of my audio collection, which can be found here, Lonely With Noise.

When I am not at home, I am usually out thrifting, having a burger, spending time with good friends, or driving somewhere new to explore with my girlfriend, Katie.

If you've got a moment, and would like to say hello or feel I may be able to help you with a project, you should say hello.
